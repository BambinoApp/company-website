Our Story

A small team with a big mission

Bambino Labs Ltd was founded with a single belief: that the tools parents rely on every day should be as thoughtfully designed as the rest of their lives.

Based in Aberdeenshire, Scotland, we are a small, independent software company dedicated to building mobile apps that genuinely help families. Every product we ship is something we'd want to use ourselves.
