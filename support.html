🚀 Getting Started
How do I download the Bambino app? ⌄

Bambino is available exclusively on the Apple App Store for iPhone and iPad. Simply search for "Bambino" in the App Store, or tap the "Download on the App Store" button on this website.

The app requires iOS 16.0 or later.

Do I need to create an account to use the app? ⌄

You can use many features of Bambino without creating an account — your data is stored locally on your device. Creating an account enables iCloud sync across multiple devices and a secure backup of your data.

Can I use Bambino on multiple devices? ⌄

Yes. If you sign in with your Apple ID, Bambino will sync your data across all your Apple devices using iCloud. This includes your iPhone, iPad, and any other iOS devices linked to your account.

Can I share access with my partner? ⌄

Family sharing features are on our roadmap. Currently, you can share your device with your partner or use the same Apple ID across devices. We plan to add multi-account family sharing in a future update.

📱 Features & Tracking
What can I track with Bambino? ⌄

Bambino helps you track the most important aspects of your baby's daily routine, including:

  • Feeding (breastfeeding, bottle feeding, and solids)
  • Sleep sessions and nap times
  • Nappy changes (wet and dirty)
  • Growth measurements (weight, height, head circumference)
  • Development milestones
  • Custom notes and reminders
How do I add a feeding entry? ⌄

To log a feed, tap the "+" button on the home screen and select "Feed". You can choose the feed type (breast, bottle, or solid food), enter the duration or amount, and add any notes. The entry will automatically be added to your timeline.

Can I edit or delete an entry after adding it? ⌄

Yes. Tap on any entry in your timeline to open it. From there, you can edit any field or swipe left to delete the entry. Deleted entries cannot be recovered, so please double-check before deleting.

Can I export my data? ⌄

Yes. You can export your data as a CSV file from Settings → Export Data. This is useful for sharing with your health visitor or GP, or for keeping your own records.

👤 Account & Data
How do I delete my account and data? ⌄

You can request account and data deletion at any time by emailing us at hello@bambino.so with the subject line "Delete My Account". We will process your request within 30 days.

You can also delete the app from your device, which removes all locally stored data immediately. If you have iCloud sync enabled, you will also need to delete the Bambino data from your iCloud storage in iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Is my data backed up? ⌄

If you have iCloud enabled, your Bambino data is automatically backed up to iCloud. This means if you get a new phone, your data will be restored when you sign in with your Apple ID and download the app.

If iCloud is disabled, your data is stored locally on your device only. We strongly recommend enabling iCloud backup to protect your records.

What happens to my data if I stop using the app? ⌄

If you simply stop using the app without deleting it, your data remains safely stored on your device (and in iCloud if enabled). You can pick up right where you left off at any time.

If you delete the app, local data is removed. Contact us if you'd like a full data export before deleting.

Does Bambino share my data with anyone? ⌄

No. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Please see our Privacy Policy for full details on how we handle your data.

💳 Billing & Subscriptions
Is Bambino free to use? ⌄

Bambino offers a generous free tier that includes all core tracking features. A premium subscription unlocks additional features such as advanced charts, unlimited history, and priority support.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription? ⌄

Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple. To cancel or manage your subscription:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top, then "Subscriptions"
  • Find Bambino in the list and tap it
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription"

You can also manage subscriptions via the App Store → your profile icon → Subscriptions.

Can I get a refund? ⌄

Refunds are processed by Apple, not by us directly. To request a refund, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's refund process.

If you believe there is an error with your billing, please also contact us at hello@bambino.so and we will do our best to help.

🔧 Troubleshooting
The app is crashing or not loading — what should I do? ⌄

Please try the following steps:

  • Force-close the app and reopen it
  • Ensure your iPhone is running the latest version of iOS
  • Check that Bambino is updated to the latest version in the App Store
  • Restart your iPhone

If the issue persists, please contact us at hello@bambino.so with a description of the problem and your device model and iOS version.

My data is not syncing between devices — how do I fix this? ⌄

Ensure the following on both devices:

  • You are signed in to the same Apple ID
  • iCloud is enabled in iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud
  • Bambino has iCloud access enabled (Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → scroll to find Bambino)
  • Both devices have an active internet connection

Sometimes iCloud sync can take a few minutes. If issues continue, please reach out to us.

I switched to a new iPhone — how do I restore my data? ⌄

If you set up your new iPhone from an iCloud backup or transferred data directly from your old phone, your Bambino data should be automatically restored. Simply download the app and sign in with your Apple ID.

If you set up as a new iPhone, install Bambino and sign in with the same Apple ID you used previously — your iCloud data will sync automatically.
